ABOUT THIS GAME

In this classic strategy game, the fate of the galaxy hinges on your tactical prowess.
Choose your allegiance, either as the resourceful and astute R2-D2 or the loyal and determined Stormtrooper, and engage in an exhilarating battle for supremacy.
Your mission is clear: connect four of your character's symbols in a row, be it horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, while thwarting your opponent's every move.

Will you join the ranks of the Empire or fight alongside the Rebel Alliance? The choice is yours in this epic battle of wits. May the best strategist triumph in this ultimate showdown between R2-D2 and the Stormtrooper!

HOW TO PLAY

Step 1

Position the mouse cursor over your token and
CLICK AND HOLD your token with your mouse or touchpad.

Step 2

While holding the token, move your cursor to the column where you want to drop it.

Step 3

Position the piece over the desired column and release the mouse button or lift your finger to drop the piece.
